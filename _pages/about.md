I am currently a Ph.D. student at the College of Future Information Technology, Fudan University, under the supervision of Professor [Xiaodong Gu](https://scholar.google.no/citations?user=-CsNYlkAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN&oi=ao). I earned my Bachelor's degree from the same institution in 2023.

My research interests lie at the Vision-Language Models (VLMs), encompassing transfer learning, few-shot learning, and domain generalization. Currently, my work focuses on AI-generated image detection, text-to-image generation, and VLA-style game agents.

I am currently a Tencent Qingyun Program research intern.
